  • We are students of the SITE faculty of ADA University, which gathered the team #9 for an ICT project. The name of our group comes from the recently coined term "Generation-Z" – abbreviated to GenZ. As our first project Hour of Code required, we had to target youngsters as our audience, so we decided to have another name for today's teens GenC that means Generation–Coding. It also resonates with the word gənc in our language, meaning "a young person". We came up with this name as a symbol of living in the century of technology. In this era of rapid development of the IT industry, it would not be wrong to contribute ourselves to "generation-coding". Also, although we have no web development experience, we have created this beginner-friendly web page for our team projects. We hope this will inspire others. We wish everyone, regardless of age, to understand that coding is not difficult, but interesting and funny. We encourage everyone to try coding at least once in their life.
